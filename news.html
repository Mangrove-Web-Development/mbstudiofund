The Manitoba Studio Fund Scholarship for 2024 is Open for Submissions

2/23/2024

Having distributed funds to six people since 2021, we are honoured to announce our fourth annual scholarship for design students of colour in Manitoba. For more information, please visit our About and Apply pages or this scholarship page on the DesCan website. Submissions must be submitted online by May 1, 2024. If you have any questions, please email us at education@descan.ca.


Manitoba Studio Fund is Open for Submissions in 2023

1/24/2023

We are very excited to launch our third annual scholarship for BIPOC design students in Manitoba. For more information about the scholarship, please visit our About and Apply pages. The application form is now open, and submissions must be submitted by May 1, 2023. If you have any questions, please email education@descan.ca.


2022 Manitoba Studio Fund Scholarship Recipients Announced

7/11/22

With DesCan Foundation, it is an honour to announce that two scholarships have been awarded to design students in Manitoba. They are Songkyung Kang (RRC Polytechnic) and Kyungdeok Park (University of Manitoba). Each will receive $2,000 to support their schooling in recognition of their design excellence and dedication to the practice. Congratulations to both of you – and thank you to all of those who submitted applications in 2022!
